The Founder

Frank Bertelot

Leader

From an early age, driven by an innate passion for clothing, our founder has embarked on a remarkable journey in the fashion realm that spans over two decades. With an unwavering devotion to the craft, he has honed his skills as a fashion designer and boasts an exceptional prowess with the sewing machine. His lifelong adoration for apparel has seamlessly translated into a thriving career, enabling him to craft innovative and exquisite designs that transcend boundaries. As a leader of our fashion house, he not only envisions captivating collections but also orchestrates a symphony of creativity, guiding teams to realize his creative vision. His role extends beyond the atelier, shaping trends, fostering an environment of innovation, and weaving a narrative that encapsulates the essence of our brand.
